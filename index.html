cozy.exchange

When you want to sell your pair of old sneakers or a new hoodie that just made, you don’t want to worry about if the buyer will pull through with his end of the deal or worry about how much money will you make at the end of the transactions.


Maybe you’re a shopper and don’t want to worry about the seller flaking or worry about if the item you bought is real or fake.

What if there was a place where you can sell clothing, new and old, without losing money on cuts. What if there was a place where you can buy clothing in person or online without complications.

What if there was a place where you can just be cozy...
